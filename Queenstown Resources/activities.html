Activities & Attractions in Queenstown

Milford Sound

Take a scenic tour to the breathtaking Milford Sound, known for its towering fjords, cascading waterfalls, and dramatic landscapes. You can cruise along the sound, go kayaking, or take a scenic flight for incredible views.

Queenstown Gardens

Located on the shores of Lake Wakatipu, Queenstown Gardens is a beautiful park offering walking trails, lush gardens, and recreational activities. You can play frisbee golf, tennis, or simply relax and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

Coronet Peak

Another excellent ski resort near Queenstown, Coronet Peak offers a range of winter activities for both beginners and advanced skiers. The resort also hosts night skiing sessions for a unique experience.

Kawaru Bridge Bungy

Another excellent ski resort near Queenstown, Coronet Peak offers a range of winter activities for both beginners and advanced skiers. The resort also hosts night skiing sessions for a unique experience.

Outdoor Activities

Queenstown is a hub for outdoor adventure enthusiasts. You can try activities like paragliding, skydiving, jet boating, white-water rafting, mountain biking, hiking, and much more.

Wine Tours

Explore the nearby Central Otago wine region and enjoy wine tasting tours. The region is known for its Pinot Noir, and you can visit various vineyards and cellar doors to sample the local wines.
